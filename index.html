F/01

Schedule-based blocking

Create windows of deep work. AppBlocker enforces them — no willpower required. Block by weekday, hour, or custom rule.

F/02

Usage limits

Set a daily budget per app. Instagram gets 20 minutes and then it's locked. Your screen time, your rules.

F/03

Focus sessions

One tap starts a focus session. Selected apps are blocked until the timer ends — no overrides, no bargaining.

F/04

100% on-device

No cloud, no tracking, no ads. Uses Android's Accessibility Service and UsageStats APIs. Your data never leaves your phone.
